Laboratory of Bioinformatics I - Module 2

The course Laboratory of Bioinformatics 1 (66563) includes in the second semester a 58-hour module given by Emidio Capriotti. The course director is Prof. Rita Casadio. The module is running from March 10 to April 30, 2020 and the lectures will be given online through the URL https://bit.ly/335F5S8. This page will include all the slides and materials of the lectures.

  • Requirements
    • This is a theoretical and hands-on module which requires the use of PC. Students need to use their own laptop. Practical session of the course will be performed on a Linux OS.
