Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks

Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks (85306) is 62-hour course of the 3rd year of the Degree in Genomics taught by Emidio Capriotti. The course is running from November 5th to December 11, 2019 in rooms and labs located in via della Beverara 123. This page will include all the slides and materials of the lectures.


  • Materials
    Course Schedule and General Information
    Protein Sequence and Structure. 5, 7 and 8 November, 2019.
    Amino Acid Properties 11 November, 2019.
    Analysis and Prediction of Protein Complexes 12 November, 2019.


  • Requirements
    • This is a theoretical and hands-on module which requires the use of PC. Students are encouraged to use their own laptop. Practical session of the course will be performed on a Linux OS.
