Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks

Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks (85306) is 62-hour course of the 3rd year of the Degree in Genomics taught by Emidio Capriotti. The course is running from November 2nd to December 11th, 2020 in rooms and labs located in via della Beverara 123. The lectures can be also attended online through the URL https://bit.ly/3oJQvFX. The Colab pages of the course are available at https://bit.ly/34SsdSe. This page will include all the slides and materials of the course.

  • Requirements
    • This is a theoretical and hands-on module which requires the use of PC. Students are encouraged to use their own laptop. Practical session of the course will be performed on a Linux OS.
