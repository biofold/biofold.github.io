Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks

Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks (85306) is 62-hour course of the 3rd year of the Degree in Genomics taught by Emidio Capriotti. The course is running from November 4th to December 14th, 2021 in rooms and labs located in via della Beverara 123. The lectures can be also attended online through the URL http://bit.do/pibn-lectures. The Colab pages of the course are available at http://bit.do/pibn-colab. This page will include all the slides and materials of the course.
