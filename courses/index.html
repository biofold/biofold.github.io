Training

Emidio Capriotti is actively promoting training in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. In collaboration with other researchers, we are working to develop a training program in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (CB2) at University of DÃ¼sseldorf.

In the 2016 summer semester the Introduction to Protein-Protein Interaction Network (iPPI) module is given as a part of the Network Biology course (M4424). This course has been organized by Prof. Martin Lercher and Prof. Markus Kollmann.

In the 2015 fall semester the Introduction to Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (iCB2) module has been offered as a part of the Introduction to Molecular Systems Biotechnology (M4453). This course is organized by Prof. Markus Kollmann.

Previously, with Malay Basu we developed the following two courses offered to the the students of the Graduate Biomedical Science (GBS) program at the University of Alabama at Birmingham.

  • CB2-101 Introduction to Scientific Computing (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti
  • CB2-201 Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti

Students with research interests in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics please contact Emidio Capriotti.
