Training

Emidio Capriotti is actively promoting training in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. In collaboration with other researchers, we are working to develop a training program in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics at the University of Bologna. Students with research interests in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics please contact Emidio Capriotti.

In 2023, I will give the Module 2 of the Laboratory of Bioinformatics I course (March 6th - April 26th) of the Master of Bioinformatics at the University of Bologna. With Sergio Decherchi, we organized the course Elements of Programming Languages for the PhD in Data Science and Computation. On the same master program, in 2022 I gave the course Elements of Biophysics (October 17-28). From November 4th to December 14th, 2021 I taught the course Proteomes, Interactomes and Biological Networks. course at the Degree in Genomics at the University of Bologna.

At the University of Duesseldorf, during the summer 2016, the module Introduction to Protein-Protein Interaction Network (iPPI) was offered as a part of the Network Biology course (M4424) organized by Prof. Martin Lercher and Prof. Markus Kollmann.

In the fall 2015 the module Introduction to Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (iCB2) was taught as a part of the course Introduction to Molecular Systems Biotechnology (M4453) organized by Prof. Markus Kollmann.

Previously, with Malay Basu we developed the following two courses offered to the the students of the Graduate Biomedical Science (GBS) program at the University of Alabama at Birmingham.

  • CB2-101 Introduction to Scientific Computing (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti

  • CB2-201 Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (3 Credit). Instructors: Malay Basu and Emidio Capriotti
